      FACILITIES


Our college has administrative rooms, faculty rooms, classrooms, laboratories, library, auditorium and hostel....










Library

Our library services are lending, reference, reprographic, and newspaper clipings. Normally 1300 volumes are under circulation. The number of books issued per day ranges between 125 & 250. The number of visitors per day ranges between 200 ..

Hostal

The hostel has excellent living rooms, a dining hall, and prayer. The accommodation maintains high standards of hygiene, round the clock security, and provides uninterrupted power and water supply as well.
  1. The Hostel is under the direct control of the Principal who is the warden. She is assisted by the Deputy Warden.
  2. Application for admission to the Hostel shall be made in the prescribed form.
  3. Hostel accommodation is not available for married students.

College Transport

The college runs 10 busses for the convenience of the students living in and around Tiruchendur. Students are permitted use the service after paying the prescribed bus fees.
