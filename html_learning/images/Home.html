GOVINDAMMAL ADITANAR COLLEGE FOR WOMEN


Welcome to Govindammal Aditanar College for Women



The year 1987 is a momentous one in the annals of Tiruchendur Taluk for it witnessed the rise of a college for women in Tiruchendur. The college owes its origin solely on the philanthropy of Dr.B.Sivanthi Adityan, the illustrious son of a noble father Mr.Si.Pa.Aditanar. The college is a self-financing institution and It is fully maintained by the munificence of Aditanar Educational Trust. No capitation fee or donation is accepted for admission or appointment. The founder of the college, Dr.B.Sivanthi Adityan was the Director of Daily Thanthi, a leading newspaper in Tamil and was a great industrialist.









Students are given academic opportunities for their lateral and vertical progress with different courses. The following are the courses offered by the college.
