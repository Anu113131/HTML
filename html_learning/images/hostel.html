Thirumathi. GOVINDAMMAL ADITANAR

Welcome to Govindammal Aditanar College for Women

  • The year 1987 is a momentous one in the annals of Tiruchendur Taluk for it witnessed the rise of a college for women in Tiruchendur. The college owes its origin solely on the philanthropy of Dr.B.Sivanthi Adityan, the illustrious son of a noble father Mr.Si.Pa.Aditanar. The college is a self-financing institution and It is fully maintained by the munificence of Aditanar Educational Trust. No capitation fee or donation is accepted for admission or appointment. The founder of the college, Dr.B.Sivanthi Adityan was the Director of Daily Thanthi, a leading newspaper in Tamil and was a great industrialist.

Chairman’s Message

Govindammal Aditanar College for Women is the first self-financing college in Thoothukudi district established by Aditanar Educational Institution in the year 1987 with the lofty idea of spreading higher education to the rural masses. Its mission is to impart quality education to the womenfolk and make them to be self-reliant, good and useful citizens of our motherland.

Principal’s Message

Our college aim is to equip the student with comprehensive knowledge by well qualified experienced staff. We encourage all our students to develop high expectations about themselves, their work and their behaviour, which is an expression of our values of Personal Best, Integrity, Respect and Responsibility.

Secretary’s Message

We promise to do everything to encourage you in furthering your aspirations. Our college provides ample opportunity to the students in acquiring the right type of knowledge and skills along with the research environment.
